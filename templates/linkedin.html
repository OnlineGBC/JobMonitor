How to get your cookie
  1. Sign in to LinkedIn in your own browser.
  2. Press F12 to open developer tools.
  3. Go to Application (Chrome/Edge) or Storage (Firefox).
  4. Open Cookies → https://www.linkedin.com.
  5. Find the row named li_at and copy its Value.

Copy the value only, not the name. It is a long string of letters and numbers.
